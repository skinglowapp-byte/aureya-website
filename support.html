Frequently Asked Questions

How does skin analysis work?

Our AI analyzes your selfie images to assess various skin metrics including hydration, wrinkles, redness, eye bags, and pore visibility. The analysis uses advanced computer vision and AI models to provide personalized insights. Important: This analysis is for informational purposes only and is not a medical diagnosis.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All images and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app settings.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data from within the app. Go to Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including photos, analysis results, and profile information.

How do subscriptions work?

Aureya offers a 3-day free trial, followed by a monthly subscription. You can manage your subscription through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

What if I'm not satisfied with the app?

We want you to be happy with Aureya! If you have concerns or feedback, please contact us at support@aureya.app. We're constantly improving the app based on user feedback.

How accurate is the skin analysis?

Our AI analysis provides estimates based on visual assessment of your skin. Results may vary based on lighting, image quality, and other factors. The analysis is designed to help you track trends over time and should be used as a guide, not a medical diagnosis.

Can I use the app without a subscription?

Aureya offers a 3-day free trial for new users. After the trial period, a subscription is required to access all features including AI analysis, personalized routines, and product recommendations.
